# Empirical Letter Meanings — Derived from Binary Nucleus Evidence
**Date:** 2026-03-24
**Method:** For each DIVERGE letter, all binary nuclei containing it were examined. Jabal's `jabal_shared_meaning` provides the empirical evidence. The common semantic thread across all nuclei = the letter's intrinsic meaning.
**Reviewer:** Claude Opus 4.6

## م (Meem) — 44 nuclei, 294 roots — HIGHEST IMPACT

### Evidence as Letter 1 (م_, 21 nuclei):
Dominant themes: **تلاصق/امتداد (adhesion+extension)** in مء,مت,مج,مد,مز,مش,مط,مع,مل (9/21). Also **خلوص (purity/extraction)** in مح,مص (2/21). **تجمع (gathering)** in مز,مم (2/21). **امتساك (firmness)** in مك,من (2/21).

When م leads, the nucleus describes something that *holds together and extends* — a continuous, connected substance or action. مد = extension to a limit. مس = contact/connection. مل = extending with encompassing. مك = firm grip.

### Evidence as Letter 2 (_م, 23 nuclei):
Dominant themes: **تجمع/ضم (gathering/bringing together)** in ثم,جم,رم,زم,سم,شم,ضم,عم,قم,مم (10/23). **اشتمال/باطن (containment/inner)** in طم,غم,كم (3/23). **احتواء (encompassing)** in خم (1/23).

When م ends a nucleus, the meaning almost always involves *gathering, collecting, or containing*. ضم = gather with pressure. جم = accumulate densely. زم = compact gathering. قم = collect into a strong mass.

### Derived Meaning: **تجمع + تلاصق (gathering + adhesion)**
The bilabial closure (lips pressing together) produces the most physically iconic letter-meaning in Arabic: pressing together, gathering, containing. As initiator, م binds and extends. As completer, م gathers and contains.

### Scholar Comparison:
| Scholar | Their claim | Match? |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| **Jabal** | امتساك+استواء+ظاهر | Partial — امتساك (firmness) is adjacent to تلاصق but misses تجمع |
| **Asim** | انتقال | Wrong — too generic, no evidence for movement as م's core |
| **Abbas** | تجمع (from bilabial closure) | **BEST MATCH** — exactly what the evidence shows |
| **Neili** | (no data) | — |
| **Anbar** | تجمع+انتقال | Good — تجمع confirmed, انتقال is secondary |

**Recommendation:** Use **تجمع+تلاصق** as م's canonical features. Abbas was empirically correct.

**Confidence: HIGH** (44 nuclei, overwhelming pattern)

## ע (Ain) — 42 nuclei, ~230 roots

### Evidence as Letter 1 (ع_, 23 nuclei):
Dominant themes: **امتداد/تلاصق (extension)** in عت,عد,عط,عم (4/23). **نقص/ظهور (diminishment/appearance)** in عر,عف (2/23). **تجمع/ضغط (gathering+pressure)** in عج,عض,عك (3/23). **غلظ/تعقد/عمق (thickness/depth)** in عص,عظ,عق (3/23). **رخاوة (softness)** in عب (1/23). **تماسك (cohesion)** in عز,عل (2/23).

Very diverse! But there's a pattern: عر (12 roots, the largest) = "stripping away the outer to reveal what was hidden" — ظهور (appearance/revealing). عن (10 roots) = "something appearing with subtlety" — ظهور+لطف. عم (9 roots) = "high fusion/gathering" — with ظاهر.

### Evidence as Letter 2 (_ع, 19 nuclei):
Dominant themes: **خروج/انتقال (exit/movement)** in بع (8 roots). **رقة/رخاوة (fineness/softness)** in ذع,رع,نع (3/19). **تفرق/تخلخل (dispersal)** in شع,صع,ضع (3/19). **قوة (force)** in عز,فع (2/19).

### Derived Meaning: **ظهور + عمق (manifestation + depth)**
The common thread is **making the hidden visible** — whether by stripping away (عر), emerging from depth (عق), or appearing subtly (عن). The letter ع connects inner depth to outer manifestation.

### Scholar Comparison:

| **Abbas** | عمق (depth) | **Good** — captures one half (depth) but misses ظهور |
| **Neili** | ظهور (appearance) | **Good** — captures the other half (manifestation) |
| **Anbar** | تفجر+إشراق+ظهور | **BEST** — تفجر=eruption, إشراق=illumination, ظهور=appearance. All point to depth→manifestation |

**Recommendation:** Use **ظهور+عمق** (manifestation from depth). Anbar was closest. Neili and Abbas each captured one half.

**Confidence: HIGH** (42 nuclei, clear depth↔manifestation pattern)

When ت follows another letter, it consistently adds a quality of *precise force* — cutting (بت), fine pressure (حت,قت), strong extension (عت,مت). The letter ت seems to mean something like "decisive directed force."
