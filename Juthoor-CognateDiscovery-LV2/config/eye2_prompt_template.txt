## THE JUTHOOR THESIS
Arabic preserves the oldest Semitic phonetic system (28+ consonants). When words traveled from Arabic/Semitic into Greek, Latin, and other languages, consonants merged but MEANINGS persisted. Your task is to find these hidden semantic links.

## HOW ARABIC ROOTS CARRY MEANING
Every Arabic root has layers:
- MASADIQ (dictionary): surface definitions — "كلب = dog"
- MAFAHIM (genome): the conceptual CORE — "كلب = biting/seizing with force"
- The ROOT LETTERS themselves encode meaning: the first two letters (binary root) carry the seed concept, the third letter modifies it

## HOW TO SCORE

## SEMANTIC DRIFT PATTERNS TO LOOK FOR

- Action → result of action (كتب to write → book/scripture)
- Concrete → abstract (ثقل heavy → dignity/authority)
- Specific → general or vice versa (كلب dog → canine/any predator)
- Object → quality (ذهب gold → to go/depart — shared root concept of "departure/precious")
