## How to Read This Table

- **Juthoor Empirical** = the letter's meaning as derived from corpus evidence, independent of scholar opinion
- **Status**: ✅ Confirmed (Juthoor agrees with dominant scholarship) | 🔄 Enriched (Juthoor adds precision) | ❌ Overridden (Juthoor corrects scholarship) | ⚠️ Low data
- **Confidence** = based on number of nuclei and clarity of cross-positional convergence
- **Closest Scholar** = which scholar's characterization comes closest to the Juthoor finding
- **Nuclei count** = total binary nuclei in which this letter appears (as either L1 or L2)

## Phonetic Group Semantic Map

Letters in the same phonetic group share semantic neighborhoods — this is one of the main structural discoveries of Juthoor LV1.

```
LABIALS (شفوية) — The Bilabial Cycle
  ب emergence/appearing   ←→  م gathering/adhesion
       ↘                        ↙
          ف separation/splitting

PHARYNGEALS (حلقية) — The Depth Axis
  ح purity/friction/dryness    خ loosening/void
  ع manifestation from depth   غ concealment in depth

EMPHATICS (تفخيم) — Force + Substance
  ص thickness+force+penetration   ض pressure+gathering+density
  ط extension+encompassing        ظ thickness+manifestation

SIBILANTS (صفيرية) — Extension Variants
  س extension+penetration+fineness
  ش spreading+dispersal

VELARS/UVULARS — Deep Force
  ك gathering+gripping+pressure
  ق force+depth

LIQUIDS (ذلقية) — Flow and Attachment
  ر continuous flow+extension
  ل attachment+extension
  ن penetration+inner+extension

LARYNGEAL
  هـ void+loosening
```

## Detailed Letter Notes

The following notes expand on the most important letters, especially those corrected or enriched by Juthoor.

### ب (Ba) — HIGH confidence, 45 nuclei

The empirical center of ب is **ظهور + خروج** (emergence + exiting). As initiator:
- بر (16 roots): التجرد والخلوص — stripping back to reveal
- بد (9 roots): الظهور والتكوين الأول — the first appearing
- بع (8 roots): الخروج والانتقال — exiting from one domain to another

As completer:
- جب (9 roots): التجسم والبروز مع القطع — corporeal protrusion with cutting
- نب (9 roots): النبو ارتفاعاً أو ابتعاداً — rising or departing

Jabal's original: تجمع + رخاوة (gathering + softness) — overridden. Neili and Asim both independently confirm ظهور+خروج.

Key examples: بدأ (to begin — first emergence), بدر (the full moon — appearing in the sky), بدا (to appear), بيت (house — a form that has emerged and stands).

### م (Meem) — HIGH confidence, 44 nuclei, CORRECTED

The empirical center of م is **تجمع + تلاصق** (gathering + adhesion). The L2 evidence is overwhelming:
- سم، عم، قم، ضم، جم، شم — all 10 major L2 nuclei carry تجمع as their first feature
- ضم (2 roots): الجمع بضغط ولأم — gathering by pressure and binding

Jabal's original: امتساك + استواء + ظاهر — overridden. Abbas's تجمع (إطباق الشفتين — lip closure = gathering) is exactly confirmed. Anbar's تجمع + تماسك + احتواء is also confirmed.

The physical reason: م is produced by closing both lips completely — gathering the entire breath into one contained point, then releasing. The body enacts its meaning.

### ع (Ain) — HIGH confidence, 42 nuclei, CORRECTED

The empirical center of ع is **ظهور + عمق** (manifestation from depth). Dominant L1 cluster:
- عر (12 roots): الظهور بعد التجرد — appearing after being stripped bare
- عن (10 roots): الظهور اللطيف في الباطن — subtle appearing from within
- عق (5 roots): التعقد في العمق — complexity deep within

Jabal's original: التحام + رخاوة + حدة (merger + softness + sharpness) — overridden. This characterization focuses on surface qualities and misses the fundamental depth-to-manifestation trajectory.

Key examples: عَرِيَ (to be naked/exposed — inner revealed), عَرَف (to know — the thing reveals itself to recognition), عِرق (vein/root — inner structure made visible), عين (eye — the organ that manifests hidden things).

### غ (Ghain) — HIGH confidence, 25 nuclei, CORRECTED

The empirical center of غ is **باطن + اشتمال** (inner + containing/concealing). Every major L1 nucleus:
- غل (9 roots): النفاذ الباطني الكثيف — dense inner penetration
- غر (8 roots): الغرور والدخول في الباطن — interior submergence
- غب (4 roots): الغئور والغياب في الباطن — sinking and disappearing inward
- غط (4 roots): التغطية والغمر — covering and submersion
- غم (4 roots): التغطية والإبهام — covering and obscuring

Jabal's original: تخلخل + رخاوة + كثافة (loosening + softness + density) — overridden. Abbas's باطن + غموض + استتار (inner + obscurity + concealment) is exactly confirmed.

Key examples: غيب (the unseen realm), غمام (clouds — that which conceals), غابة (forest — dense concealing interior), غلّ (hidden hatred).

### هـ (Ha light) — VERY HIGH confidence, 30 nuclei

The هـ has the most unambiguous derivation in the entire dataset. **65% of all L2 nuclei** carry فراغ (void) as their primary feature — the highest single-feature specificity in the alphabet.

L1: هـم (void + dissolution), هـر (collapse + loosening), هـد (demolition)
L2: جهـ, رهـ, بهـ, تهـ, سهـ, نهـ — all describe various forms of void or emptying

Physical: the هـ is pure breath — the lightest possible articulation, a movement of air through an unobstructed channel. The body produces void; the letter means void.

### ن (Nun) — VERY HIGH confidence, 52 nuclei, most in alphabet

The ن displays a unique **bidirectionality**: as initiator it penetrates outward; as completer it enters inward.
- L1 dominant: نف (14 roots) = النفاذ الخارج — penetrating outward
- L2 dominant: سن، عن، حن، زن، كن، جن، خن — all end in باطن (inner)

This is the "needle-and-womb" duality: both a needle (penetrating outward from a point) and a womb (receiving inward to depth) are described by ن. نار (fire — penetrating energy), نهر (river — penetrating flow), أنين (moan — sound from inner depths), نفس (soul/breath — the inner self that also flows outward).
